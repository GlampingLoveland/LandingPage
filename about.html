About Us

🌿 A Dream Rooted in Nature, Family, and Innovation 🌍

What if you could wake up to the sound of birds singing, breathe in the crisp mountain air, and gaze at an endless sky—without sacrificing comfort, technology, or the warmth of home?

Glamping Loveland was born from a dream—a vision for a new way of living and experiencing the outdoors.

We are an Italian family with a deep passion for innovation, nature, and adventure. We love to explore, travel, and discover new ways to embrace outdoor living. And above all, we love animals. We believe that every experience in nature is more meaningful when shared with them.

But how many times have outdoor and animal lovers been forced to choose between comfort and freedom?
We refuse to choose.
For us, life has always been a balancing act—between dreams and reality, between the need for stability and the desire to build something bigger. Like many, we find ourselves caught in the daily routine, where work and responsibilities push dreams aside.
But we refuse to let that be the end of our story.
We want to create a place where adventure, technology, and nature come together—without compromise. This is why we are building Glamping Loveland.
A place that is more than just a business idea—it’s a new way to experience tourism, exploration, and a deep connection with animals and the environment.

🏕️ What makes us different?
✔ Eco-friendly geodesic domes, surrounded by breathtaking landscapes
✔ Smart home automation & sustainable energy solutions for a seamless experience
✔ Unparalleled stargazing experiences, with telescopes and expert-led events
✔ A deep respect for nature and animals, welcoming four-legged guests with open arms
✔ Adventure tours & outdoor activities for hikers, explorers, and adrenaline seekers
✔ A cutting-edge innovation hub for testing new outdoor technologies

For us, this is more than a business.

It’s a proof of concept—a way to show that a more conscious, tech-driven, and sustainable lifestyle is possible. It’s the fulfillment of a dream for our family, but also an opportunity for anyone who longs to experience nature in a whole new way—through adventure, discovery, and freedom.
That’s why we are launching a crowdfunding campaign. Not just to fund the project, but to build a community of explorers, nature lovers, and visionaries—people who believe in change, innovation, and the future of outdoor living.
If you’ve ever dreamed of a place where adventure meets comfort, where the future of outdoor experiences takes shape, and where every stay feels like a glimpse into a better, more sustainable world—now is the time.


🔗 Join us. Be part of this journey. Help us make Glamping Loveland a reality.
